Project Overview

A comprehensive certificate management platform designed to help schools efficiently manage student certificate registration, review, and archiving. The system provides separate admin and student portals for complete certificate lifecycle management.

🏢
Admin Portal
Certificate Management & Approval
👨‍🎓
Student Portal
Certificate Inquiry & Registration

Features

📜
Certificate Management
Support for three certificate types: government, professional, and campus certificates with CRUD operations
👨‍🎓
Student Management
Student information maintenance with Excel batch import and custom class/college settings
✅
Application Review
AI intelligent pre-review + manual review, automatic score verification
📚
Grade Archiving
Class-based certificate tracking with attachment upload support
📖
Training Materials
Publish training information and study materials with category filtering
🤖
AI Chat Assistant
Intelligent Q&A for students to get answers about registration and requirements
